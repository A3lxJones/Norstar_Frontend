Team Photo (600x400)

About Norstar Inline Hockey Club – Ballymena Norstar Inline Hockey Club is a community-focused inline hockey club based in Ballymena, Northern Ireland, dedicated to developing and promoting the exciting sport of inline hockey for players of all ages and abilities. The club operates out of the Ballymena North Business and Recreation Centre, where members train, play, and grow together in a fun, inclusive, and supportive environment.

Founded in 2022, Norstar quickly established itself as a hub for grassroots inline hockey, welcoming players from across the Ballymena area and beyond. As an affiliated club within Inline Hockey Ireland, Norstar fields junior teams in national competitions and actively participates in the growth of the sport across the island.

Norstar’s mission is to introduce young people to inline hockey through accessible programmes like “Learn to Skate,” offering coaching, equipment access, and opportunities to develop fundamental skating and hockey skills. The club prioritises inclusivity, teamwork, and sporting enjoyment, with a strong focus on helping members achieve personal growth both on and off the rink.

Norstar also emphasises community engagement, hosting events, camps, and activities to bring families and local supporters together. With dedicated volunteer coaches and a thriving junior section, the club continues to build a lasting legacy for inline hockey in Ballymena and the wider region.
